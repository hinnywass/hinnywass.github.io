Forrest Yoga

Forrest Yoga is a strong and dynamic form of vinyasa yoga which is specifically designed to counteract the discomforts of modern life. Hinny is Oxford's first Forrest Yoga teacher, and she offers public classes and private lessons.

Violinists' Yoga

Specialist class to help develop more resonant tone, freedom of creative expression and better posture. Ease aches and heal injuries, calm performance anxiety, and find a happier relationship with your instrument.

Lunchtime Office Escapes

A 30 minute Forrest-inspired class to stretch out your muscles, refresh your brain, nourish yourself with deep breath and get your energy moving!

Forrest Yoga meets every student where they are at right now today, and helps them strengthen, lengthen and heal.

Hinny says, "I am hugely passionate about Forrest Yoga, and I am very excited about the possibilities it offers to every single human being to become happier, healthier, stronger and more comfortable. It is a powerful tool for change and growth, and no matter how fit, how bendy, how strong you are I'm certain you'll find that life is always better with yoga in it!"
